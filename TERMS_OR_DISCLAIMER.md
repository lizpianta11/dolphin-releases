## 2. What Dolphin does (and does not) do
- Dolphin **passively observes network packet *metadata*** (protocols, addresses,
  ports, sizes, timing). It runs locally and an on-device AI (OTORO) explains what
  it sees.
- Dolphin **does not block, filter, or modify traffic.**
- Dolphin is **not** antivirus, **not** EDR, **not** a firewall, and **not** a
  guarantee of security. It surfaces evidence from observed metadata; it cannot
  prove a device or network is safe, malicious, or compromised.
- Dolphin does **not** send your captured data to the cloud and collects **no
  telemetry** in this beta. See [PRIVACY_POLICY.md](PRIVACY_POLICY.md).
